I’ve been fortunate enough to grow up in a household who values entrepreneurship and owning your own business. Growing up, I’ve seen how my parents had to cut corners and sacrifice on various services due to owning a small business. It’s not always easy competing with some of the bigger names in your industry or community.

Unfortunately during the 2020 pandemic, small businesses are being hit the hardest. I have found a lot of small businesses cannot afford a custom website that is built with their business model in mind. That’s why I believe in the $0 down, $150 a month business model. You’ll get a custom built, tailored design for your business, with UNLIMITED updates, for a manageable cost.

From one small business to another, we understand where you're coming from!

FAQ's

What exactly am I paying for?

You're paying for a personal web designer/developer who is carefully looking after your site and ensuring it's running to it's fullest potential, making you more money. Our services include building you a custom site that is built specifically for your business, having us on-call to make any minor changes to your website at any time, hosting your site, as well as constant updates with new web standards.

Why are "hand-coded" sites superior to page builders such as Wix, Squarespace, etc.?

When you hand-code a site, it runs exactly how you want it to run. There is no extra code that bloats up your website, making it run slower. Websites built by hand will out-perform most Wordpress sites and drag-and-drop page builders. The more organized your code is, the faster your site will run. We diligently write line by line of code to ensure your site is running to it's fullest potential.

What do you mean by "unlimited customizations?"

If you need to update your hours, messaging, add new images, etc., we can do that for you! Maybe you're a restaurant and want to update your weekly menu, we can do that! Maybe you're a contractor and want to add more services on your site, we can do that. We reserve the right to deny any customizations that are outside the scope outlined prior to beginning your site.

What do you need from me to get my site up and running?

All we need from you is whatever content you may need for your site as well as your domain. If you don't already own your own domain, we can help you purchase it. A typical 5 page website includes your home page, an about page, services, portfolio (or gallery/images), and a contact page. If you want to swap out one of those pages for something else you have in mind, we can work with you. We will also ask for your logo and any type/color preferences. If you would like to give your company a fresh look with an updated logo and identity, let us know.

What happens if I cancel?

Not happy with our services? Not a problem! You can cancel at any time. Once you cancel, your site will be down unless you resume our services again. You get to keep your domain (the url) and your content (the information living on your website). If you would like to keep your website after cancelling, you can purchase the design & code from us for a one-time fee.

What's the catch?

There is no catch! When we say we care about our local community and small businesses, we really do mean it. Once you sign the contract and pay for the first month, we get to work and build a custom site for you. Your site should be ready in about 2 weeks, once we receive all the content from you.
